How Does Rebel Reviewer Work?

While our primary audience is Ole Miss students, anyone is welcome to look at the reviews people have created on our website for any business in Oxford, Mississippi

But because this website was made by and for Ole Miss affiliates, only current Ole Miss affiliates can leave a review.

Simply click on one of the business pages, scroll/search to find a particular business/businesses, view their ratings and if you have an account, feel free to leave a review about your experience there. If approved, the review will be live for everyone to see.
